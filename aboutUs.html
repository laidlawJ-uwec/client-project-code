Our Story

Electron Emporium was founded in 2004 with the goal of providing high quality mechanical and electrical parts for professionals and hobbyists alike.

How We Ship Products

Products are shipped with trusted carriers such as FedEx, USPS, UPS and more. Items that need additional precautions, such as batteries, are clearly labelled and specially handled to prevent damage.

FAQ

Q: When will my items arrive?
A: It will depend on shipping destination, but estimated arrivals can be checked using the "Services" page.

Q: What kind of services are provided?
A: We do repairs for computers, parts that we sell, or any other product that we have the means to restore to proper functionality. We charge for parts and labor, like most other repair venues. We assign dedicated and trained servicers to every request.

Q: What do I do if I have an issue with a purchase? A: Recent purchases (less than 30 days) can be shipped back and replaced with no additonal charges. Otherwise, follow the details listed under a purchased product warranty for replacement or repair options. Specialty or custom orders cannot be returned or exchanged.
